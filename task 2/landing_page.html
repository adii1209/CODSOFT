ABOUT US

Meet FreshHarvest, a group of passionate individuals with decades of experience in growing and selling fruits. Combining their knowledge of the fruit industry with modern technology, they launched an online platform to bring fresh, high-quality fruits directly to customers' doorsteps. FreshHarvest started when the team realized there was a growing demand for fresh and exotic fruits that many people couldn't easily find in local stores. They decided to bridge this gap by creating an online marketplace that offered a wide variety of fruits, sourced directly from the best farms. The team has established relationships with trusted farmers and suppliers to ensure they offer only the highest quality fruits. They personally inspect farms and orchards to select the best produce, ensuring freshness and taste. FreshHarvest's website is designed to be user-friendly, making it easy for customers to browse through the selection of fruits, read descriptions, and place orders. The site is regularly updated with new arrivals and seasonal specialties. To maintain the freshness of the fruits, the team has set up a robust logistics network. They use temperature-controlled storage and quick delivery services to ensure that the fruits reach customers in perfect condition. FreshHarvest places a strong emphasis on customer satisfaction. They have a dedicated customer service team that handles inquiries, resolves issues, and provides personalized recommendations to ensure a positive shopping experience.

VARIETIES

Apple

Apples are renowned for their unique look, taste, and vibrant color. Grown and shipped form the ideal temperate climate of Kashmir, they offer a delightful crunch and sweetness that is unmatched.

Mangoes

Mangoes, often referred to as the "king of fruits," are cherished for their juicy sweetness and rich flavor.We offer a diverse range of mango varieties, including the renowned Alphonso, known for its exquisite taste and aroma

Grapes

Grapes, grown extensively in Maharashtra, are prized for its sweet flavor, making it a favorite for both eating fresh and winemaking. We cultivate a variety of types, including seedless and black varieties.

Pomegranates

Pomegranates from India, especially those grown in Maharashtra, are celebrated for their vibrant red seeds and sweet-tart flavor. Known for their rich juice and health benefits, they are a prized fruit both domestically and internationally

Oranges

Oranges from India, particularly the famous Nagpur oranges, are cherished for their juicy sweetness and vibrant color. They are renowned for their refreshing taste and are enjoyed fresh or as a tangy addition to culinary dishes.

Bananas.

Bananas, widely grown across India, are appreciated for their convenience, nutritional value, and versatility in culinary applications. They provide a quick energy boost and are a staple in many Indian diets, enjoyed both raw and in various dishes
